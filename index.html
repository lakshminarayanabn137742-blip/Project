Courses Offered

Engineering

Computer Science Engineering (CSE) deals with design, development, and testing of computer systems and applications. It encompasses areas like software engineering, data structures, computer networks, and artificial intelligence. CSE graduates can work as software developers, data scientists, network administrators, and more. It offers a wide range of career opportunities in tech industry.

Management

Management involves planning, organizing, and supervising business operations to achieve goals efficiently. It encompasses areas like finance, marketing, human resources, and operations management. Effective management enables organizations to maximize productivity and achieve success. Managers play a key role in decision-making and leadership.

Computer Applications

Computer Applications involves studying computer systems, software, and applications to solve problems and manage data. It covers areas like programming, database management, networking, and web development. The field enables development of efficient software solutions and IT systems. Graduates can work as software developers, system administrators, and IT consultants.
